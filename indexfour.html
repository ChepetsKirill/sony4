PS4

The PlayStation 4 (abbreviated PS4) is a stationary game console from Sony Interactive Entertainment LLC (until April 2016: Sony Computer Entertainment Inc.). It was unveiled as the successor to the PlayStation 3 at a Sony press conference on February 20, 2013. It was launched on November 15, 2013 in North America and November 29, 2013 in Europe, South America and Australia. The PlayStation 4 is in direct competition with Microsoft's Xbox One, as well as Nintendo's Wii you and Nintendo Switch. The PlayStation 4 is the sixth PlayStation-branded game console, excluding hardware revisions. On October 8, 2019, the successor PlayStation 5 was announced, which finally appeared in November 2020.
